# Sunny Street Notes

This plugin is scoped to Sunny Street Digital Being workflows, but the fixture
must not copy proprietary Sunny Street assets.

Guidance:

- Use generated/stub fixture assets for examples.
- Keep adapter notes descriptive until the Sunny Street runtime import contract
  is stabilized.
- Record animation names, sheet paths, frame size, frame order, and anchors in
  the manifest.
- Keep provider claims in `capability-matrix.json` explicit: active, deferred,
  or unavailable.
